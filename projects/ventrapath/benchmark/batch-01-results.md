## Results

### VP-P0-004 — United States — premium home maintenance membership

- Specialist phase: Usable
- Specialist ceiling/runtime observation: Not instrumented in this run
- Bob synthesis runtime: Not instrumented in this run
- Pass/fail: Pass
- Reason: Strong service business with clear homeowner pain, recurring demand, and a defendable twist once narrowed to a preventive home-care membership built around a living home-maintenance record plus coordinated seasonal service windows.
- Commercial notes:
  - Blueprint landed as a premium continuity service, not a generic handyman marketplace.
  - Best twist was the homeowner-facing maintenance ledger that tracks filters, gutters, seasonal tasks, property quirks, and upcoming service windows across vendors.
  - Market logic was credible around time-poor, higher-income homeowners who value prevention over reactive repairs.
  - Monetisation held up through membership plus booked-service margin, with the main risk being operational complexity if coordination is promised too broadly.

### VP-P0-005 — Australia — kids activities discovery and rebooking platform

- Specialist phase: Usable
- Specialist ceiling/runtime observation: Not instrumented in this run
- Bob synthesis runtime: Not instrumented in this run
- Pass/fail: Pass with intervention required

- Specialist ceiling/runtime observation: Not instrumented in this run
- Bob synthesis runtime: Not instrumented in this run
- Pass/fail: Pass
