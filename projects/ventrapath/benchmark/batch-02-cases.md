# Batch 2 Cases

## Scope

Run these 6 Phase 0 benchmark cases and record pass/fail outcomes plus useful notes.

## Cases

### VP-P0-006
- Country: Singapore
- Industry: food and beverage
- Idea: A service that helps office managers run rotating team lunch ordering from local vendors with budgets and dietary preferences handled automatically.

### VP-P0-007
- Country: United Kingdom
- Industry: property
- Idea: A service that helps landlords manage small repair jobs, tenant reminders, and contractor coordination without using heavy property software.

### VP-P0-016
- Country: United Kingdom
- Industry: construction
- Idea: A quoting and job-flow platform for independent builders handling leads from WhatsApp, Facebook, and referrals.

### VP-P0-017
- Country: Canada
- Industry: mental performance
- Idea: A program that helps managers avoid burnout and improve energy using AI check-ins, accountability, and small group support.

### VP-P0-026
- Country: United Kingdom
- Industry: creator tools
- Idea: I want to help creators stop burning out and run the business side better, maybe with an AI assistant, maybe with people, maybe with templates.

### VP-P0-027
- Country: Canada
- Industry: family logistics
- Idea: Something around helping separated parents coordinate kids, schedules, and communication, but without making it feel like legal software.
