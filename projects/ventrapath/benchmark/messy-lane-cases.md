# VentraPath Messy Lane Cases

## Purpose

This lane tests whether VentraPath can honestly rescue or reject fuzzy free-text ideas.

## Cases

1. **VP-P0-024** — overwhelmed mothers AI/community/support concept
2. **VP-P0-025** — teen careers with AI/mentors/community
3. **VP-P0-026** — creator burnout/business-side support hybrid
4. **VP-P0-027** — separated parents coordination without legal-software feel
5. **VP-P0-028** — elder-support AI/community/content/membership hybrid
6. **VP-P0-029** — green home upgrade marketplace/advisory/network ambiguity
7. **VP-P0-030** — local business messages/calls recovery tool without CRM drift
8. **VP-P0-031** — diabetes support with reminders/family/coaching ambiguity
9. **VP-P0-032** — youth jobs/community/business-model ambiguity
10. **VP-P0-033** — family travel AI + experts concierge/software hybrid

## What this lane should prove

- VentraPath can narrow fuzzy ideas without bluffing.
- VentraPath can reject weak concepts for the right reason.
- Honest rejection is treated as success when rescue would require inventing a different business.
