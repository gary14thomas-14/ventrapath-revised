# VentraPath Hook Test Cases

## Purpose

Test whether the general Phase 0 output is attention-grabbing, concise, UI-fit, and exciting enough to pull a user forward.

This is not a rescue/failure test. It is a **hook quality** test.

## Cases

### HT-001
- Source: VP-P0-002
- Idea: A service that helps small trades businesses like plumbers, electricians, and roofers turn WhatsApp conversations into booked jobs, quotes, reminders, and follow-ups.

### HT-002
- Source: VP-P0-018
- Idea: A lightweight operations layer for small Shopify brands that turns scattered support and fulfilment issues into one action queue.

### HT-003
- Source: VP-P0-010
- Idea: A service that helps small clinics reduce no-shows using reminder flows, rebooking prompts, and waiting-list fill tools.

### HT-004
- Source: AI ice cream store test
- Idea: AI ice cream store in New York.

## Scoring focus

- Does the Business brief hook attention fast?
- Is the twist visible early?
- Does it feel like it belongs in the VentraPath UI?
- Is it concise enough to keep momentum?
- Would it make a user want to continue?
