# VentraPath Blueprint Lane Cases

## Purpose

This lane tests whether VentraPath can turn already-legible business ideas into strong, UI-fit blueprints.

## Cases

1. **VP-P0-004** — premium home maintenance membership
2. **VP-P0-006** — office lunch ordering ops flow
3. **VP-P0-007** — landlord repair and contractor coordination service
4. **VP-P0-010** — clinic no-show recovery flow
5. **VP-P0-012** — small agency WhatsApp hiring workflow
6. **VP-P0-014** — creator back-office operations desk
7. **VP-P0-016** — builder quoting and job-flow platform
8. **VP-P0-018** — Shopify exception/action queue
9. **VP-P0-020** — small law firm intake and follow-up workflow
10. **VP-P0-021** — tutoring-centre WhatsApp trial and renewal flow

## What this lane should prove

- The output hooks well on good inputs.
- The twist appears early.
- The blueprint feels like the real VentraPath UI.
- Commercial clarity stays strong without overexplaining.
