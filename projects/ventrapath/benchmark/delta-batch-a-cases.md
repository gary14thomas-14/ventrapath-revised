# VentraPath Delta Batch A

## Purpose

Re-test a small set of previously weak, failed, or borderline cases **after** the new blueprint rules were added:

- blueprint must fit the VentraPath UI
- opening business brief should be short and punchy
- unique twist must be embedded into the business brief
- sections should stay concise and momentum-friendly

Goal: see whether these changes improve output quality on the hardest cases.

## Cases
