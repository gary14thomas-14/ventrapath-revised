# Batch 4 Cases

## Scope

Run these 6 Phase 0 benchmark cases and record pass/fail outcomes plus useful notes.

## Cases

### VP-P0-010
- Country: Ireland
- Industry: healthcare admin
- Idea: A service that helps small clinics reduce no-shows using reminder flows, rebooking prompts, and waiting-list fill tools.

### VP-P0-011
- Country: Germany
- Industry: pets
- Idea: A membership for cat owners that bundles sitting visits, medication routines, and travel-time care coordination.

### VP-P0-020
- Country: Ireland
- Industry: legal admin
- Idea: A client-intake and follow-up workflow service for small law firms that lose leads through inconsistent response and manual scheduling.

### VP-P0-021
- Country: India
- Industry: education / tutoring
- Idea: A service that helps tutoring centres convert WhatsApp enquiries into paid trial sessions, renewals, and attendance reminders.

### VP-P0-030
- Country: Ireland
- Industry: local business ops
- Idea: A tool for small businesses to stop losing customers in messages and calls, but I do not want it to be just another CRM.

### VP-P0-031
- Country: India
- Industry: health / habits
- Idea: Something around diabetes support with AI reminders, family accountability, and coaching, but I do not know how clinical it should be.
