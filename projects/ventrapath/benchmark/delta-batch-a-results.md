# VentraPath Delta Batch A Results

## Scope

Re-test of 6 previously weak cases using the current rules:
- shorter business brief
- UI-fit structure
- twist embedded into the business brief
- tighter, momentum-friendly sections

Method used per case:
1. Blueprint Architect pass
2. Differentiation Strategist pass
3. Market Intelligence pass
4. Monetisation Architect pass
5. Bob synthesis only if specialist output was usable

## Results

- new result: Conditional pass
- changed?: no

- Specialist usability verdict: Usable

**Current Bob synthesis verdict**

- Commercial read: better presentation, same underlying business quality.

## Batch summary

- improved cases count: 2
- unchanged cases count: 4
- worsened cases count: 0
- overall read: The new rules did improve performance on a small subset of weak cases, but mostly where the core issue was drift and generic framing. On the hardest vague consumer/community ideas, the gain was mostly presentation only. The new rules help the system lock a sharper wedge sooner, but they do not magically solve payer ambiguity or weak commercial boundaries.

## Bottom line

The current short/UI-fit rules are useful, but not universally transformative.

They materially improved:
- VP-DELTA-004
- VP-DELTA-005

They mostly improved presentation rather than business sharpness:
- VP-DELTA-001
