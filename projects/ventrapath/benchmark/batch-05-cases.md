# Batch 5 Cases

## Scope

Run these 6 Phase 0 benchmark cases and record pass/fail outcomes plus useful notes.

## Cases

### VP-P0-012
- Country: India
- Industry: recruitment
- Idea: A lightweight hiring workflow tool for small agencies handling WhatsApp candidate chats, interview scheduling, and follow-ups.

### VP-P0-013
- Country: South Africa
- Industry: automotive
- Idea: A service that helps busy drivers manage vehicle servicing, repair quotes, pickup logistics, and reminders through one membership.

### VP-P0-022
- Country: Germany
- Industry: workforce training
- Idea: A platform that helps skilled trades apprentices track practice, get coach feedback, and stay on top of certification milestones.

### VP-P0-023
- Country: New Zealand
- Industry: wellness services
- Idea: A membership that helps freelancers stay healthy using preventive physio routines, posture coaching, and accountability check-ins.

### VP-P0-032
- Country: South Africa
- Industry: jobs / skills
- Idea: I want to build a community that helps young people get work using AI, peer support, and training, but I am not sure what the business model is.

### VP-P0-033
- Country: New Zealand
- Industry: travel / families
- Idea: A family travel planning thing with AI and experts that handles itineraries, kids, meals, and stress, but I do not know if this should be software or concierge.
